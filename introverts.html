Respecting introverts.

It is often annoying to few people who cannot understand why there is a need for people to be alone. Life isn’t the same for all.All our fingers aren’t the same size. The same goes with people around the world.The world is composed of vivacious people,grumpy people,people who often complain,contentious people or people who are prone to negativity .The other set of people include introverts,extroverts and ambiverts. Imagine how a world would look with same kind of people everywhere? Indeed,it would be boring and hopeless.

There are people who enjoy hanging out with friends, who like partying all the time, who like being swamped with people around them. On the other hand there are also people who like being alone, who are awkward in groups and calm. Their thoughts are antipodal. But the boundaries between these thoughts is debilitating due to the society. The society looks down upon introverts. It is absolutely ridiculous to expect introverts to come out of their comfort zones and mingle with people around them.This is because people are always comfortable in their own skin. Every person’s opinion must be left to his/her discretion. This kind of rational thinking must be developed in people. Introverts’ silence or awkwardness is often mistaken to arrogance. Extroverts are seen as magnanimous,ebullient, welcoming and affable people. Whereas introverts are misconstrued to be “taciturn”, ”arrogant” , “demure” , “supercilious” , traits that imply smallness of personality or character.

This might be one of the reasons why extroverts are dominating public life. Most of the people refuse to make an attempt to understand introverts who decline banter and who are not into lively conversations. Every person’s choices and opinions must be equally respected as this isn’t antisocial and it is not a sign of depression. The world becomes a better place only if all its people are treated equally.
