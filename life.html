Life is short.Live life to the fullest.

It is quite intimidating to know and remind ourselves that life is short. It is even more hassling to know that we spend most of our life in dismay or whining over our problems and clinging on to the past. But life isn’t this after all.

Life is an epitome of completeness and amusement galore. It throws both challenges and opportunities. The way we take it is left to our discretion. We shouldn’t be melodramatic and get carried away by the hurdles that stand on our way. Instead we must be bold to face them with resolution.

We shouldn’t wait till we get older. We must be quite enthusiastic to explore the life and find out what life has in store for us. We should live every minute like they were the last minutes of our life. Never let our fears obscure our thoughts and supersede our desires. Fear is inevitable and it can destroy us. Never succumb to fear. We ought to fight the challenges that life throws at us in a hope that someday we can overpower it.

We must take decisions wholeheartedly and find happiness in every little thing we do. We must purge negativity and stop being presumptuous. We shouldn’t be overwhelmed when we encounter a fiasco as life is full of possibilities. Failure is just the beginning of new hope.
